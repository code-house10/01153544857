    SERVICES

    Office & Household Local moves

    Your assigned dedicated moving consultant, will walk you through every step of your move, answering all your questions. You don’t have to lift a finger on moving day, our expert packers will handle all the details, down to the last spoon. We treat every object like it’s a personal memory of inestimable value to you. 

    International Door to Door Service

    Moving abroad is an exciting time and you shouldn't need to worry about the logistics of shipping your goods. With our 35 years of experience in international moves, we'll take care of your door-to-door shipment from A to Z, so that you can focus your attention on the fun aspects of your move.

    Warehousing

    Limited space is a common problem for many people, our warehousing and storage facilities take care of this problem for you. Whatever it is you want to store, there is a safe and secure space for it in our secure network of warehouses which are of the highest industry standards.

    Packing & Crating

    Packing is an art and our packing procedures have advanced it to a near- science. You may save some money by doing part of the packing yourself, but on some items, the possible saving in self-packing doesn’t make up for the risk you take with improper packing.

    Transportation

    We will transport your carefully packed items from one point to another. Before your move even begins, we have worked out all aspects of handling your goods securely from departure point to arrival point.

    Customs Clearance

    Clearing Customs requires extensive experience and knowledge of Customs regulations. It also depends on the trust built over the years between our brokers and Customs officials. Our brokers are always in the know when it comes to required documents or any changes to customs regulations.
